# Code of Conduct

Witness South Africa (WSA) is a non-racial, constitutional, non-violent,
truth-first movement. This repository is an extension of that movement.
If you contribute here, you agree to behave in a way that is consistent
with the movement's principles.

## Our standards

We expect contributors and maintainers to:

- Engage with evidence, not insult. Argue the point, not the person.
- Treat every contributor with dignity, regardless of race, class,
  religion, language, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age,
  or political affiliation.
- Accept correction and give it with care. Everyone is learning.
- Protect the identities and safety of witnesses, whistleblowers, and
  vulnerable members of the movement. Never post identifying detail about
  a third party without their explicit, informed, written consent.
- Keep disputes off social media. Raise issues in the repo or through
  the private channels listed in `SECURITY.md`.

We will not tolerate:

- Slurs, harassment, threats, or dehumanising language directed at any
  person or group.
- Doxxing, including the publication of real names, addresses, phone
  numbers, ID numbers, or family details of any contributor, witness,
  target of reporting, or public figure beyond what is already a matter
  of public record and necessary for the accountability work.
- Incitement to violence or celebration of violence.
- Partisan campaigning on behalf of, or against, any political party
  in South Africa. The platform is non-partisan by design.
- Sock-puppetry, coordinated inauthentic behaviour, astroturfing, or
  the use of this software to impersonate real people.

## Scope

This code applies in every repository space: issues, pull requests,
discussions, code comments, commit messages, review comments, and any
community channel (Signal, WhatsApp, mailing list, meeting) that carries
the WSA name.

## Enforcement

Reports go to the Cause Council at `conduct@witnesssouthafrica.org`
(to be set up). The Council will acknowledge receipt within 72 hours and
respond within 14 days. Possible responses range from a private warning
to a permanent ban from all WSA spaces and the removal of all contributor
privileges. Enforcement decisions are recorded (redacted of complainant
detail) so the community can see that the code is applied consistently.

## Our creed

> I will stand, speak, document, organise, and endure — and I will stay
> alive and free long enough to matter.

This is attributed to the movement's founding statement. If this is not
the posture you are bringing, this is not the community for you.
